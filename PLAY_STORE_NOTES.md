# PLAY STORE NOTES — CleanCalc

## App Description (Paste into Play Console)
CleanCalc is a fast, offline calculator for everyday arithmetic. It supports decimals, continuous calculations with operator precedence, and clear/delete controls. CleanCalc runs entirely on-device with no ads and no data collection.

## Privacy Policy Template
**Privacy Policy for CleanCalc**

CleanCalc is an offline calculator application. The app does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal or device data. All calculations are performed locally on your device. We do not use analytics, tracking, or third-party SDKs. If you have questions, contact the developer.

## Adaptive Icon Replacement (Optional)
If you want to replace the launcher icon with a custom 512×512 PNG:
1. Place the PNG here: `/app/android/branding/launcher_512.png`
2. In Android Studio: right‑click **app** → **New** → **Image Asset**.
3. Select the PNG as the foreground layer and choose a background color.
4. Click **Next** → **Finish** to regenerate adaptive icons in `/app/android/app/src/main/res/`.
